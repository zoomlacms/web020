Enter China market

China has become the largest emerging market in the world after a continued opening-up and economic development over the past three decades, and has a huge demand on imported motor vehicles and machinery. Meanwhile, not all top-selling products in its home country could be imported into China and delivered to end users, as China have different industry policies, technical regulations, governmental ruling, and infrastructure and consumption behavior. Dynaso has been doing business in these areas for many years and accumulate extensive knowledge and experience. We provide value added service in areas of project feasibility study, risk assessment, project management and implementation, for importation as well as localization.
