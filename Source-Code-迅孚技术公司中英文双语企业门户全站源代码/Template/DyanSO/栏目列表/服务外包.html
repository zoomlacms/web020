Homologation Agent Service

We provide unrivaled expertise in areas of automotive regulations and homologation, our service includes certification scenario planning, cost evaluation, application and data submission, facilitation of samples delivery and testing, factory audit preparation and field services, etc.

We have established a very strong professional relationship with relevant governmental authorities, certification bodies and testing laboratories across the country. Our clients can obviously benefit the followings from our service:

1- a more cost-effective certification plan
2- smooth communications with authorities
3- lower risks deriving from certification and compliance
4- expediting the product homologation process
5- product marketing time framework ensured
6- being timely informed of the latest development of future regulations
7- drawing on other clients’ experience and lessons
