Regulation & Homologation

Motor vehicles have a significant impact on the safety of citizen, environmental protection and energy saving, all major countries, including China, have established relevant legislations, technical standards and homologation systems, based on its national conditions. It is pivotal for entrepreneurs to have access to and understand the market entry policies and requirements in order to ensure a risk-free product launching and delivery, and sustain the business growth.

Dynaso has built extensive internal and external resources, information channels and database, allowing us to provide our clients with latest regulations, technical standards and homologation rules of China and other major markets all around the world, based on their product offers and business plans.
