CEO Message

Dynaso was founded in 2005 at a time when China joined the World Trade Organization and accelerated the integration into the world economic system. Since setbacks and challenges have come up on market access and local business growth for both international companies entering the China market and Chinese companies expanding overseas market in the early time. Dynaso has seized this opportunity to timely adjust the business direction to define our market value as helping clients smoothly launch products in the target market, effectively reduce the risks from industrial policies and products in conformity with the standards and assist the clients to improve the products competitiveness in the target market.

In the last decade, Dynaso and clients have witnessed and benefited from the rapid development of China automobile market. Meanwhile, I am proud of Dynaso’s growth from a startup with only 2 staff to a developing professional service company with branches in several cities, cultivating a cross-cultural understanding and communication professional team. Our clients come from Fortune 500 automobile and parts companies, as well as small-scale luxury brands companies; large-scale trading and service enterprises, as well as government professional institutions and associations. From this year, we begin to provide market research and consultant service regarding application of Connected Vehicles to large insurance firm.

There is no doubt that our clients are richly experienced in international trade or investment under different cultural and economic backgrounds. They finally choose Dynaso as partner or service provider, because Dynaso deeply comprehends the responsibilities to clients, fulfills the professional and behavior standard expected by clients, and establishes the brand reputation and reliability, which is also the basis that Dynaso exists.

Our purpose is to help business owners, entrepreneurs and executives achieve the success they want for themselves and their businesses. Our focus is on giving our clients the tools they can apply to build their business and solve business problems for years to come. We always feel grateful for the trust of clients; we constantly stick on the business concept and ethics of “Being your trusted partner – building successful clients and employees.” to create values for clients and help staff to develop. This is the only way we can achieve our grand vision and become an admirable and valuable professional service company.

Thanks for your attention and support!


June 23,2015
